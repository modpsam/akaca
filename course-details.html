Course Description

The Amazing Klef Academy is an intensive 8-week physical program designed to help passionate individuals become skilled and confident photographers. Through a hands-on, real-world approach, students will master their cameras, understand lighting, direct visual stories, and deliver polished work.

From day one, we’ll dive into the fundamentals, creative direction, lighting techniques, post-production, and the business of photography. You won’t just learn how to take pictures – you’ll learn how to build a brand, work with clients, and stay relevant in a fast-paced creative industry.

Upon completion, you should be able to:

  • Shoot in manual mode and adapt to different lighting conditions
  • Direct creative projects and work smoothly with models and clients
  • Retouch professionally and meet industry standards
  • Understand pricing, contracts, and grow a sustainable creative business
