About Us

The Academy

The Amazing Klef academy provides physical and virtual photography / creative experiences for individuals in Nigeria, Africa and across the world.

If you are looking to begin your journey in the world of photography, develop your photography skills, learn the creative/business side of photography or refine your skills, we have a class for you.

What makes the Academy unique is; courses are broken down and taught in single classes so instead of teaching you photography from start to finish; we teach you what you really need to know. So from a "Retouching masterclass" to a "Business of Photography Workshop" and an "Advance Portrait Masterclass" you get to choose what you really want to learn and register for that.

Our photography classes are designed to cater for a broad category of creatives in the beginner, intermediate, and professional level.

The Founder

Amazing Klef is a Nigerian Photographer & Multifaceted Creative. He is best Known for his creative imagery & is among the handful of Creatives in Nigeria that have worked with the crème-dela-crème of the industry and also an impressive number of Global & National brands.

He has been crafting a niche for himself in the creative industry since the young age of 18 using different mediums from visual art to graphic design, photography, cinematography & more. He is also well known for his catch-phrase 'AmazingOrNothing' and is popularly reffered to as “Color Boy” because of his creative use of colors.

Amazing Klef is widely recognised for bringing a youthful and urban feel to the creative industry & he is also widely celebrated amongst young creatives as a pioneer & influencer.

His Portfolio boasts of visual works/collaborations with/for: Monster Energy, Oraimo, MTN, Fairmoney NG, Hennessy, ASUS, Pepsi, Star Radler, OPPO Mobile, Smoov Chapman, Legend Extra Stout, Trophy Lager, BBCNews, GTBank, Tecno Mobile, Mavin Records & other recognised brands including SMEs & Multinationals.

He has also worked with notable personalities including; Davido, Burna Boy, Rema, Buju, Toke Makinwa, Nancy Isime, Fisayo Fosudo, Tayo Aina, Ckay, Blaqbonez, Oxlade, Rudeboy of Psquare, Laycon, Zlatan, DJ Khaled, Ice Prince, 9ice, Phyno, Sarkodie, Broda Shaggi, Reekado Banks, DJ Spinal, Kizz Daniel, Zoro, DJ Kaywise, Solidstar, Skiibii, Harrysong, Yung6ix, Fiokee, DJ Coublon and an endless list of superstar in the entertainment, creative & professional field.
