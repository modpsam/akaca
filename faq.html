Who is the Academy for?

Amazing Klef Academy is for photographers, creatives and visual storytellers who are ready to go from raw talent to professional execution - whether you’re a complete beginner, looking to refine your skills or rekindle your creative flame.

⁠Do I need experience to join?

No. Our Beginner’s Course is designed for individuals with no prior experience - including those who have never handled/seen a camera before.

⁠Is this an online or physical academy?

It’s a physical academy based in Lekki, Lagos - Nigeria. All classes & training happen in-person at our creative space.
But we also have a digital store www.theamazingklefstore.com for virtual courses & digital products.

Who teaches the classes?

All classes are facilitated by Amazing Klef and a team of working creatives and professionals in the industry.

⁠What should I bring to class?

If you own a camera or laptop, bring it. If not, we’ll guide you on how to start with what you have. Some gear is provided for practical sessions.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes — but more importantly, you’ll leave with knowledge, creative boost and all the skills you need to execute professionally.

How long is the program?

Our programs run at different times throughout the year, each one intensive, hands-on, and carefully structured to deliver results.
